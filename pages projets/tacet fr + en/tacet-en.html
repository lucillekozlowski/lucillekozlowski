Feedback - Tacet n°4

Editorial work concerning  feedback and experimental
notions for the fourth publication of Tacet, a sound arts
and experimental music magazine. The project is based on the glitch
process, which allows generations of random visual deformation.
In addition, a moiré effect adds visual games to the reading.
The choice of color and fabrication are reduced in order
to bring a certain clarity to the final form.
