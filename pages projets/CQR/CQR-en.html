../../ Lucille Kozlowski

Ce qui reste

A project based on ideas of family.
I have chosen to talk about transmission,
the things which remain and which become part
of our self-definition. The notion of memory
is rendered using the technique of Cyanotype,
its permanent impression reflecting the longer term.
The innocence of childhood is evoked through line
drawings. The images are untitled to allow
subjective interpretation and intelligibility.

------------------------------------------

publication and cyanotype, sept. 2015 - january 2016
