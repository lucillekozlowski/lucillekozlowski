Normographes

The freeing of letters from typographic rules
using the random cutting of paper shapes.
Each shape cute was determining the next cut.
These forms, manipulable materials and generators
of graphic experimentation, are organized in templates
defined according to their tendency : angles, ellipses,
waves. These templates provide a completely free
technique for building images and letters.
