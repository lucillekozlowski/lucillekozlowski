La mouette libre

Propositions designed for Matéo Langlois, young producer of music.
His work is caracterized by a mix between urban poetry and songs,
and also performances on the scene. These images are proposing
urban constructions and a work on pictural touch, based on a work
of lights, which are a part very important of the musical atmosphere
of Matéo's compositions.
