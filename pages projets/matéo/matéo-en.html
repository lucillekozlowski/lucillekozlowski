La mouette libre

Propositions designed for Matéo Langlois, young musician.
His work is caracterized by a mix between urban poetry and french song.
These images are proposing urban constructions and a work
of the pictural touch, based on lights which are a part very important
of the musical atmosphere of Matéo's compositions.
