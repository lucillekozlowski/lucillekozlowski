Mémory

A project developed as part of a workshop
organized by the second years of the masters degree.
Participants had to pick up a card listing some characteristics
and functions of an object and to imagine a new form for it.
I picked up « retaining information / memory / chance »
and « memory development ». I decided to work on a concept
dealing with memory, adding a playful dimension as the game
Memory does. Every piece-card is divided into two forms
painted in the same pattern. The small size of these items
allows a compactness and creates a collection.
